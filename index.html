Biography

Dr. Manmohan Singh born September 26, 1932, Gah, West Punjab, Indian economist and politician, who served as prime minister of India from 2004 to 2014. A Sikh, he was the first non-Hindu to occupy the office.Singh attended Panjab University in Chandigarh and the University of Cambridge in Great Britain.
